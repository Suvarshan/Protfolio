# How to Add Your Resume

To enable the resume download feature:

1. **Get your resume PDF ready** - Convert your resume to PDF format

2. **Place the file in the public folder:**
   - Locate: `my-portfolio/public/`
   - Add your resume file as `resume.pdf`

3. **The download button will work automatically!**

Your resume is now ready to be downloaded by anyone visiting your portfolio.

## File Structure:
```
my-portfolio/
├── public/
│   ├── vite.svg
│   └── resume.pdf  ← Add your resume here
├── src/
└── ...
```
